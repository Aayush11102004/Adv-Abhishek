CYBER LAW

Cyber law, also known as internet law or cybercrime law, refers to the legal framework governing the use of the internet and online activities. It encompasses a broad range of issues related to technology, data protection, online privacy, and electronic commerce.

CIVIL LAW

Civil law is a branch of law that deals with disputes between individuals or organizations. Unlike criminal law, which focuses on offenses against the state, civil law addresses conflicts between parties where one party seeks compensation or specific performance rather than punishment.
